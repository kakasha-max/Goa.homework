Sedan

Sedans are known for their comfort and fuel efficiency. They are perfect for city driving and long road trips. Sedans typically have a three-box configuration with separate compartments for the engine, passenger, and cargo. Popular models include the Toyota Camry, Honda Accord, and BMW 3 Series.

SUV

SUVs offer a higher driving position and more space. They are great for families and off-road adventures. SUVs come in various sizes, from compact to full-size, and often feature all-wheel drive or four-wheel drive capabilities. Popular models include the Ford Explorer, Jeep Grand Cherokee, and Toyota Highlander.

Sports Car

Sports cars are designed for performance and speed. They are perfect for those who love driving fast and looking stylish. Sports cars often feature powerful engines, sleek designs, and advanced handling capabilities. Popular models include the Porsche 911, Chevrolet Corvette, and Ferrari 488.

Electric Car

Electric cars are the future of transportation. They are eco-friendly and offer a smooth and quiet ride. Electric cars are powered by electric motors and batteries, which can be recharged at home or at public charging stations. Popular models include the Tesla Model S, Nissan Leaf, and Chevrolet Bolt.

Luxury Car

Luxury cars offer the highest levels of comfort, technology, and performance. They are designed to provide a premium driving experience with high-quality materials and advanced features. Popular models include the Mercedes-Benz S-Class, BMW 7 Series, and Audi A8.

Classic Car

Classic cars are vintage vehicles that have stood the test of time. They are often restored and maintained by enthusiasts who appreciate their historical significance and timeless design. Popular models include the Ford Mustang, Chevrolet Bel Air, and Volkswagen Beetle.
